Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Carlsbad Chamber of Commerce

This name represents a city where the chamber operates, providing resources for local businesses and the community.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to:

  • Promote business in the Carlsbad region by offering business resources and networking opportunities.
  • Serve as a community hub for news, events, and policy updates.

Scenarios

Possible user questions driving the content development:

  • What events are coming up for local businesses?
  • How can I contact the chamber for business opportunities?
  • What is the local business growth and economic climate in Carlsbad?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the site will include:

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #FF9800 (Orange) - for buttons and highlights.

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif
  • Body Font: 'Arial', sans-serif
